Matt is a research associate in the Dynamics Research Group, based in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Sheffield. His research is focussed on combining Bayesian machine learning techniques with physical knowledge in order to model the dynamic behavior of structures such as bridges, wind turbines, aircraft components, and other high value assets. Applications include detecting and localising damage for monitoring the health of structures, and force identification for fatigue estimation.
